Welcome to Glow

Glow focuses mainly on skin improvement with specialties in acne, anti-aging and permanent make-up, but you are also welcome for a relaxing massage.

In the salon you can enjoy treatments in a pleasant atmosphere that are completely tailored to your needs and wishes. We aim for the best quality in terms of skin improvement and skin care.

Prior to treatment, an intake is conducted and a skin analysis is made. We will then discuss with you what it takes to make the skin shine. This makes a visit to Glow a relaxing experience.

We would like to welcome you when you visit our salon.

Kind regards,
Romina ten Hoope and Danielle Pijnaker

About Us

Romina

My name is Romina ten Hoope, a certified beautician. I obtained my diploma as a beautician in 2014. I am certified in acne, permanent make-up, skin improvement massage and camouflage. You can come to me for skin care, skin improvement, but also for a relaxing treatment.

Danielle

My name is Danielle Pijnaker, all-round beautician. I obtained my diploma in 2005, after which I specialized in permanent make-up, microdermabrasion and skin improvement. I completed my nutritionist diploma in 2015. I started following this course because I often saw a link between skin problems and nutrition in practice. Of course you can also come to me for a wonderfully relaxing treatment.
